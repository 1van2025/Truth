In Code We Trust

Is your truth THE truth?

Empowering Public Discourse Through AI-Moderated Debate

Introduction

In modern democracies, crucial policy debates often happen without meaningful public participation. While social media has given everyone a voice, it hasn't provided the tools to make those voices effective or impactful. Major policy decisions affecting millions - from healthcare to education to economic policy - are frequently made without proper public discourse or understanding of what citizens truly want and why.

Problem Statement

  • Limited platforms for structured policy debate
  • Difficulty separating informed opinions from noise
  • Echo chambers preventing idea refinement
  • Corporate and special interest dominance
  • Missing infrastructure for collective intelligence
  • No clear path for good ideas to rise to the top

Mission Statement

"In Code We Trust" aims to empower the public voice by creating a platform where important policy debates can be refined, elevated, and amplified through structured competition and collective intelligence. Our mission is to transform public discourse from noise into signal by helping people develop and demonstrate the strength of their arguments.
